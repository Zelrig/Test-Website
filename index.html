Things To Do!

St. Augustine has something for everyone — from pirates to lighthouses to beaches and more!

⭐ 4.7
🏰
Castillo de San Marcos
Historic Fort · Must Do!
A giant real fort built in the 1600s right on the water! Walk the walls, see cannons, and learn cool history. Kids absolutely love it — especially climbing up the walls for amazing views!
📍 Downtown 👶 Baby Friendly 9AM–5PM
⭐ 4.7
☠️
Pirate & Treasure Museum
Pirate Fun · All Ages
The whole family will LOVE this! Interactive exhibits, real treasure chests, free tours from an actual dressed-up pirate, and kids can do a treasure hunt map. Super fun for everyone!
📍 Near the Fort 🏴‍☠️ Kids Love It 10AM–7PM
⭐ 4.7
🔦
St. Augustine Lighthouse
Landmark · Scenic Views
Climb 219 steps to the top for amazing views of the whole city and ocean! The grounds are gorgeous with huge old oak trees. Great for photos and totally worth the climb!
📍 Anastasia Island 🌊 Ocean Views 9AM–6PM
⭐ 4.5
⛲
Fountain of Youth Park
History · Family Fun
Drink from the legendary Fountain of Youth! The park has FREE ROAMING PEACOCKS walking around everywhere — the baby will go crazy for them! Great for grandma too, very relaxed pace.
🦚 Free Peacocks! 👴 Grandma Approved 9AM–5PM
⭐ 4.8
🏖️
Crescent Beach
Beach Day · So Pretty!
Voted one of the best beaches on the East Coast! Super clean, barely crowded, beautiful soft sand. Great waves for playing in — the baby and whole crew can spend all day here easily!
🌊 Gorgeous Beach 🅿️ Free Parking Open 24hrs
⭐ 4.8
🕹️
Anastasia Arcade Museum
Retro Arcade · So Fun!
Pay ONE price and play ALL the classic arcade games all day — old school Pac-Man, pinball, you name it! Perfect for cousins and the whole family. Totally unique and so much fun!
🎮 Free Play All Day Fri–Sun only 11AM–8PM
⭐ 4.6
🪖
Colonial Quarter
Living History · Super Cool
Step back in time! Live demonstrations, blacksmiths, musket fire, and a tower with amazing views of the fort. Guided tours are hilarious and informative. Great for everyone in the crew!
📍 Downtown 💥 Real Cannons! 10AM–5PM
⭐ 4.4
🛝
Project Swing Park
Outdoor Play · Free!
A huge awesome wooden playground right near the fort — totally FREE! Great for running around, burning energy, and relaxing on the benches while the little ones play. Perfect afternoon stop!
🆓 Free! 👶 Baby Friendly 7:30AM–7PM
🍽️ Eat Up!

Where To Eat!

St. Augustine has amazing food — fresh seafood, fun vibes, and places the whole crew will love!

🦞
Harry's Seafood Bar & Grille
Seafood · Cajun · Waterfront
Right on the water downtown with live music! Amazing Cajun food, great for the whole family. The shrimp creole and crab soup are legendary. Everyone always loves this place!
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ $$ · Great Value
🐠
Beachcomber
Seafood · Beach Bar · Casual
Right on the beach with your toes in the sand! Super chill vibes, great fish tacos and wings. Perfect for a laid-back lunch after a morning at the beach. The kids will love the outdoor tables!
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ $$ · Beach Vibes
🌮
Treylor Park
Funky American · Unique Menu
Super unique and fun menu — steak egg rolls, amazing fish & chips, Miyagi tacos. The staff is super friendly and the whole place has a great energy. Everyone in the crew will find something they love!
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ $$ · Super Fun
🍳
The Blue Hen Café
Breakfast · Southern · Cozy
The BEST breakfast spot in St. Augustine! Fresh fluffy biscuits, incredible chicken & biscuits, and the friendliest staff. Perfect for a big family breakfast to start the day. Get there early — it gets busy!
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ $ · Super Affordable
🌊
Aunt Kates
Seafood · Riverside · Classic
Hidden gem right on the river with gorgeous views! Huge portions of fresh seafood, amazing crab cakes, and beautiful scenery. Great for a special family dinner. Grandma will absolutely love the atmosphere!
⭐⭐⭐⭐ $$ · Worth It!
🍦
Ice Cream on St. George St!
Dessert · Strolling · Fun
St. George Street is the main historic street — walk around, shop, and grab ice cream! Tons of little sweet shops, perfect for the baby and everyone else after exploring downtown St. Augustine!
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ $ · Can't Miss It!

💡 Pro Tips

Good To Know!

A few handy tips to make the trip even smoother for the whole crew!

☀️
Pack Sunscreen!
Florida sun is NO JOKE — bring lots of sunscreen especially for the baby and grandma. Reapply every 2 hours at the beach!
🌊
Beach Gear
Bring a beach umbrella, towels, and sand toys for the baby. Crescent Beach is the best — not too crowded and super clean!
🚗
Parking Tip
Downtown parking can get busy! The parking garage near Castillo de San Marcos is your best bet. About $5 after 5PM!
🍽️
Make Reservations!
For big restaurants like Harry's and Aunt Kates, call ahead especially with a big crew of 8+. Will save you a long wait!
👶
Baby Essentials
808 Bounce and Project Swing Park are amazing for the little one. The Fountain of Youth Park is super stroller-friendly too!
📸
Take Lots of Photos!
The lighthouse, the fort, the peacocks, and the beach sunset — this trip is going to make amazing memories. Document everything!
