Adventurer's Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gen Con? ▼
Gen Con is the largest tabletop gaming convention in North America. Founded in 1968 by Gary Gygax, co-creator of Dungeons & Dragons, it has grown into a 4-day celebration of board games, RPGs, card games, miniatures, and the people who love them.
When do badge sales open? ▼
General badge sales open in February 2026. VIP packages go on sale in January. We recommend signing up for our newsletter to get notified the moment sales open — badges typically sell out within days.
Are children welcome? ▼
Absolutely! Gen Con is a family-friendly event. Children age 12 and under receive free entry with a paying adult. We have a dedicated Family Gaming Hall with age-appropriate games and learn-to-play sessions throughout the weekend.
Do I need to register for specific events? ▼
Many events are free with your badge and available on a first-come basis. However, ticketed events (tournaments, special seminars, workshops) require separate event tickets purchased during registration. Event registration opens in May 2026 for all badge holders.
What is the Free Play Library? ▼
The Free Play Library is our legendary game-lending collection of over 3,000 titles available to all badge holders at no extra cost. Check out any game, grab a table, and play — it's one of Gen Con's most beloved features and runs all four days.
Can I buy games at Gen Con? ▼
Yes! The Exhibit Hall features 600+ vendors selling games, miniatures, accessories, art, and Gen Con-exclusive items. Many publishers debut their newest games at Gen Con first. The hall is open Thursday–Saturday 10AM–6PM and Sunday 10AM–4PM.
Is cosplay allowed? ▼
Cosplay is not just allowed — it's celebrated! Gen Con features a costume contest, cosplay meetups, and you'll find hundreds of incredible costumes throughout the convention. Please review our prop and costume policy on the full FAQ page.
